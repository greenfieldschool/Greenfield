Greenfield School Policies (Staff Section)
Continuous Assessment
* Subject notes, class participation, weekly tests and Final examination constitute the GCS assessment method
* The soft copy of the question should be submitted to the administration examination board for approval immediately after resumption. Otherwise forfeit holiday salary payment.
* Every subject teacher should check back to make sure his/her examination questions have been approved or to make corrections where necessary.
Starting and Closing time
* Staff's resumption time is on or before 7:15 from Monday to Friday. 
* Academic staff are expected to close 3:30 pm from Monday to Thursday and 1:30pm on Friday.
* Staff on duty are expected to close 4:00 pm from Monday to Thursday and 2:00pm on Friday.
* Admin staff are expected to close 5:00 pm from Monday to Thursday and 3:00pm on Friday.
* The workshop starts at 7:15 am and ends at 7:45 pm prompt.
Dress code
* Wears should always be neat 
* Males should be dressed in shirt, trousers, socks, shoes and tie on Monday, Tuesday and Thursday. School sports shirt is worn on Wednesday. Native wear on Friday.
* Females should be dressed in shirts, skirts, shoes on Monday, Tuesday and Thursday. School sport shirt and Hair should always look neat and decent
* You can mix up Greenfield colors which are green and yellow with neutral colors such as white and black in your dressing.
Activities
* General staff workshop runs on Monday, Wednesday and Friday. 
* The welfare and academic committee meeting runs on Tuesdays and Thursdays.
* Staff should be punctual to the place of their official assignment.
* Staff should maintain exemplary moral conduct wherever they are assigned to.
School Pass
* A month's notice at least should be given to the management before access to a pass.
* Urgent/Emergent situations like illness should be reported immediately to the school management. Doctors’ reports or related documents to the event should be attached with a pass letter.
Rewards
* Salaries shall be paid to staff every last working day of each month.
* GCS organizes an occasion called Teachers shower to ceremonially recognize its staff.
* GCS also recognizes outstanding staff at the end of session events.
Class Management 
Teaching time management
   * Settling the class down 5 min
   * Review of past work/New topic introduction 10min
   * Body of the topic(s) 30min
   * Conclusion 5min
   * Assignment/C.A. 10min


Class Arrangement
Students are arranged in the classes according to the alphabetical order of their names or their heights. Class indicators must be well displayed. Educational charts in relevant subjects must be visible in all classes


Performance Rating
1. Evaluated based on effectiveness in academic and welfare issues
2. Evaluated based of the drive of the staff member towards organizations success
Inventory Management 
* Twice in a term inventory must be carried out.
* accountant/Admin. Officer must sign the inventory documents and file.


Communication 
Effective communication via writing, verbal, whatsapp, telephone calls, SMS, etc.


General
Notice Board
Reports, timetables, roasters, award recognition, person of the week, advertisements and urgent announcement are placed on the noticeboards
With Colleagues
Staff must have good cordial relations with one another.
Committee Meeting
* Meeting of the officials of the academic and welfare committee reports submission twice a month.
* Occurs every Tuesday and Thursday of the week from 7:15 – 7:45 am at an approved location, minutes of the meeting should be well documented.
* Discussions on going about the day to day activities, revaluation, and reporting of committee activities is achieved during this meeting must be recorded.
Workshop Meeting
* Meeting of all member staff except those on official duties
* Occurs every Monday and Friday of the week from 7:15 – 7:45 am at an approved location
* Report of what went on, why and when during the week is discussed and reported and recorded during the meeting
* Proposal for the upcoming weeks are agreed upon in this meeting from both the academic and welfare sections


Management
* The heads of the welfare and academics respectively shall fill in weekly reports to be submitted to the upper management
Media Platform
* Teachers shall communicate with each other through WhatsApp or their emails platform approved by the management for information that promotes the best interest of the organization
Other Places
* Communications among members of Greenfield Comp. Schools done in other places shall be in accordance with Greenfield Schools’ policy on code of conduct.
With Learners
Classroom
* Teachers shall ensure they use proper tone and attitude when speaking to learners.
* Teacher shall use Queen’s English as their baseline for communication
* Teachers should ensure every member of the class is carried along during the course of communication.
* Grading of pupils/students should be well displayed in the classroom e.g gold, silver, bronze.
Assembly
* Assigned teachers shall communicate directives and reports that were concluded during staff meetings to be communicated to learners. Five minutes of educational message must be passed during each morning assembly.
Media Platform
* Teachers shall  communicate with learners through the admin using media platforms such as bulk sms, web portal, phone call, WhatsApp or emails platform approved by the management for information that promotes the best interest of the organization
With Parents
Parent Teacher Association (PTA) Meeting
* Date of meeting is communicated to all members at least two months before meeting through all platforms
* Members of the meeting constitutes all registered teachers, guardians, parents and well wishers of Greenfield School
* This meeting shall be organized once in every term
* The venue of this meeting shall be in the school premises
* Reward point will be given to parents in attendance of the P.T.A meeting
Communication Book
* Year coordinators should send daily reports of learners’ progress to their parent or guardian through Greenfield’s communication book
* Guardians are expected to input their signatures and feedback, and send back to year coordinator 
* Proper inspection by welfare heads weekly to ensure efficiency of this medium 
Events communication 
4 communications for every event 
* Date of event is communicated to all members at least two months before the event through all platforms
* Reminder is done one month before the event 
* Reminder for a week before the event 
* Reminder for 2 days before the event 
* Reminder for on the day of the event

Between Students
Classroom
* Learners shall ensure they use proper tone and attitude when speaking to their fellow learners.
* Learners shall use Queen’s English as their baseline for communication
* Communication in other languages such as French or Yoruba shall be done during their respective period or activities.
Assembly
* According to an approved roaster, assigned learner’s representative shall come up to communicate necessary information on the assembly ground


Employment 
* New staff shall be placed on an initial three-six months probation subject to performance rating
Learners Government
* Learners shall elect their representatives in a free and fair manner
* Representatives shall give timely feedback to learners and school management
* New government shall be re-elected at the end of every academic session
* Former government shall hand over required knowledge and documents needed when there’s a transition in governance
________________
Event
Organization procedure for an event:
1. Event site preparation: Sitting arrangement, decoration, Advertisement materials 
2. Refreshment
3. Edutainment: Drama students, choreography etc
Indoor Event
Early Morning Activity (EMA)
* This activity runs from 7:15am to 7:45 am every working day.
Teachers
Teachers are expected to put in early morning activities for their learners according to their agreed roaster at the end of everyday or early in the morning so that learners can complete their EMA when they arrive early in the morning.
Learners
Learners should ensure they complete their EMA everyday as this forms part of their performance rating
Assembly
* This should start 7:45 am every working day
* In the first 10 minutes, all members of Greenfield School except those on duty are grouped into Muslim and Christian sections for early morning prayer.
* The anthems are recited in 3 minutes
* The announcement is done by the student representative or teacher depending on the approved term assembly roaster for 9 minutes
* Conclusion is done by the assembly prefect and/or student representative for 3 minutes
* Learners march into their classes in an organized manner in two minutes
* This activity is expected to end by 8:05 am every working day
* The primary and secondary learners are expected to conduct their assembly at separate locations as approved by the management

        (D) Academic and Social Club

(E) Quiz and Debate
* Either a quiz or a debate shall be organized once in every two weeks depending on the approved term calendar or roaster.


Binding Clause
This policy is binding on every member as stated
Amendment of this policy must be effected only by the school management 


Exceptions Clause
This policy is an exclusive list, if it is not in the policy, it shouldn’t be done. If it is very important, the policy should be reviewed by the relevant stakeholders
